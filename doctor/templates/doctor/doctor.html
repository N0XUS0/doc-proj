About Dr. Khaled El Sayed

Dr. Khaled El Sayed is a highly experienced gastroenterologist with over 35 years of practice in digestive system diseases. He holds a PhD from Cairo University and is a consultant in gastroenterology and endoscopy procedures. Dr. El Sayed's clinic in Mansoura is equipped with the latest medical technology including modern endoscopy equipment and ultrasound devices.

With a patient-centered approach, Dr. El Sayed provides comprehensive care for conditions such as GERD, IBS, Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis, and liver disorders. His expertise in diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopy procedures makes him one of the most sought-after specialists in the region.
